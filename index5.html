Possible Solutions To Tackle
The Issue Efficiently

1. Replace with Renewable Energy – limits the burning of fossil fuels

2. Consume Less – mind your carbon footprint

3. Apply 3R – reuse, reduce & recycle whatever you can

4. Drive Less – move closer to work or use public transportation

5. Go Vegetarian – good for health & environment

6. Stop Cut Down Tree – stop the carbon dioxide emissions

7. Unplug – stop wasting electricity unnecessary

8. Switch To Hybrid or Electric Car - renewable energy
